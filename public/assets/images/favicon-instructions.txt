FAVICON SETUP INSTRUCTIONS
========================

To complete the favicon setup, you need to:

1. Replace the placeholder favicon.svg with your actual logo
2. Create the following favicon files from your logo:
   - favicon.ico (for older browsers)
   - favicon-16x16.png (16x16 pixels)
   - favicon-32x32.png (32x32 pixels) 
   - apple-touch-icon.png (180x180 pixels for iOS)

Recommended tools:
- Online: https://favicon.io/
- Online: https://www.favicon-generator.org/
- Desktop: GIMP, Photoshop, or any image editor

Current files:
- favicon.svg (placeholder - replace with your logo)
- site.webmanifest (PWA manifest - ready to use)

Your favicon should be simple and recognizable at small sizes.
The car emoji (P) in the current placeholder represents "ProviSor".
